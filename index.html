Places where AEW has been



All Elite Wrestling was founded in 2019. It's inaugural event was their Double or Nothing Pay-Per-View event, which took place at the MGM Grand Garden Arena in Las Vegas, Nevada on May 25, 2019.

Here's all the places AEW have been to since then. The most visited states were Florida, Illinois, and Texas. Most of the AEW Dynamite events have taken place in the Eastern Central and East Coast.

AEW will be returning to the West Coast for their Double or Nothing Pay Per View event on May 23rd, 2020 which will be held once again at the MGM Grand Garden Arena in Paradise, Nevada.

Wednesday Night Wars



AEW's Dynamite debuted on the TNT network on October 2, 2019. Since then, AEW and WWE's NXT have been battling for viewership Wednesday nights with Dynamite airing on TNT and NXT airing on USA Network. While it is debatable to whether Nielson ratings are still a relevant performance indicator, there is still interest in comparing the viewership and Nielson ratings between WWE NXT and AEW Dynamite.

WarnerMedia announced on January 15th, 2020 that TNT's AEW Dynamite has been renewed through 2023. In addition, they will also be launching a second series on another night, details to come.

Recently, Cody Rhodes spoke in an interview about the future of AEW and the potential of moving the show to a different night to compete against a difference WWE brand. Link to the interview can be found here.

Ratings data obtained from ShowBuzzDaily (http://www.showbuzzdaily.com/)
Note that AEW Dynamite did not air on December 25, 2019.






Data



Data was retrieved from Cagematch The Internet Wrestling Database (https://www.cagematch.net/). The scraping was performed using Python, Beautiful Soup, and Splinter. The data was then written to JSON files and imported into Tableau for analysis.

All matches aired on Pay Per View events, Dynamite, and Dark are counted in the data. This includes unsanctioned matches, battle royals, and unofficial matches. Note that this differs from how AEW officially computes their rankings.
